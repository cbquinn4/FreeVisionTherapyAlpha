Eye Exercises

Try these simple eye exercises to help improve focus, coordination, and reduce eye strain:

1. Divergence

Train your extraocular muscles

2. Near and Far Focus

Hold your thumb about 10 inches from your face and focus on it for 15 seconds, then shift your focus to something 20 feet away for another 15 seconds. Repeat 5 times.

3. Eye Circles

Sit comfortably and slowly move your eyes in a circular motionâ€”clockwise, then counterclockwise. Repeat 10 times each direction.

4. Palming

Rub your hands together to warm them, then gently cover your closed eyes with your palms without pressing. Relax and breathe deeply for a minute.

These exercises can help reduce digital eye strain and improve your visual comfort.
